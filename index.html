background

All Arakan Youth Organizations Network was founded by Rakhine Youth New Generation Organization in 2012, since the networking and role of youths were not powerful, with the aim to build the capacity of youth and promote the role of youth by strengthening the participation of youth in social, politics as well as community development of the country; and working on community development capacity development of youth, encouraging youth for networking by collaboration with partner organizations from different area and government departments. The network was now strongly composed of 23 networks from 15 townships, 5 steering committees, 46 committee members (2 per organization), including from youth organizations initiated by the All Arakan Youth Organization Network as well as the other youth networks from different organizations. In collaboration with local and organizations such as Oxfarm, Scholar Institute, Capacity Building Initiative (CBI) and ActionAid Myanmar (AAM), AAYON has provided training and workshops to enhance leadership and advocacy skills. AAYON is working with member youth organizations to strengthen and to build a sustainable development capacity to lead their own communities in Rakhine State with the power of youths. Every year did celebrated anniversary (youth forum) in AAYON, at time all participants from 15 township Rakhine State was attend we discussed to work for the year by breaking group. As a result, AAYON are working on Youth and gender, Youths and peace and Youths and capacity. And we met regularly every three months at one time, Four times in each one year.
